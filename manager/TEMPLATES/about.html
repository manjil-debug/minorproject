About Us

 
We specialize in the creation of exceptional and customized wedding events, we design, plan and manage every project from conception to execution. We bring dreams to reality!
 
The name ‘Shanqh’ is inspired from an Indian instrument that is played on commencing any auspicious occasion, and we carry the motto of ‘Building dreams to reality’. Eventure excels in Wedding Planning and has covered more than 5+ Wedding Planning projects in Kathmandu and out of valley.

 

 
Our group of professionals understand that breakthrough ideas are only as good as the discipline supporting them. At Eventure we believe that there is a sublime connection between the mastery of management and the creation of creative ideas, most importantly, the intangible – element of surprise to make every Wedding a different and a seamless experience.
